Funding  • Jul 5th '22

We need your help to fund our brand!

We are a start-up company focused on taking care of all the busy mothers who are busy taking care of their kids. With your help we want to ensure the future of our high-quality company!

As a start-up company, funding is always important. We have interviewed moms and tested our products until we were satisfied, and now that we are there, we need your help to lift our company from the ground! In order for ezmom to unlock its fullest potential we need crowdfunding. With our funds we plan to grow our community to help even more mothers all over the world, and you can help us do that!

Of course, ezmom has picked out special gifts for our generous donors, and there are three different categories, depending on the amount that you donate.

Tier number one is from €5 and up we offer an honorable mention on our ezmom site. Ezmom takes pride on its community, so your honorable mention will be highly apricated by other moms!

Tier number two is from €25 and up. Within this tier we offer a special test care box. This care box can give you a little taste of what our care boxes have to offer.

Tier number three is for our greatest donors, and it starts from €50 and up. Not only do these donors receive a luxurious test care box, but they also get a poetry eBook!

We appreciate every single donor, no matter how big or small your support is. Ezmom is not ezmom without you, and we welcome you to our community with open arms.
