Funding  • April '23

We need your help to fund our brand!

We are a start-up company focused on taking care of all the busy mothers who are busy taking care of their kids. With your help we want to ensure the future of our high-quality company!

As a start-up company, funding is always important. We have interviewed moms and tested our products until we were satisfied, and now that we are there, we need your help to lift our company from the ground! In order for ezmom to unlock its fullest potential we need crowdfunding. With our funds we plan to grow our community to help even more mothers all over the world, and you can help us do that!

Tiers? What’s that! Well, in the Merriam-Webster dictionary “Tiers” are defined as “a row, rank, or layer of articles. Especially: one of two or more rows, levels, or ranks arranged one above another.” But honestly, that sounds so stiff to us.

The reason we incorporated a tier system in our start-up is because we care about accessibility. We want our content to be able to reach the audience that matters to us the most. You’re not made from money, and neither are we. We understand that. Because of that, we want to prioritize mindfulness in everything we do. We value transparency and honesty over everything, and we want you to be a part of our community because you want to and because you chose to.

To those who decide to join our tiers, thank you for your support. To those who don’t, we understand, and we are still grateful you took your time to be here.

Thank you for taking the time to get to know us and to see what we’re all about. Moving forward we hope to become a part of your life, and we hope to become the solid support system you might be looking for.

We appreciate you all to the moon and back. It really does take a village.
