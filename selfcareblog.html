The Importance of Self-Care for Moms

As a mom, it can be difficult to prioritize self-care when there are so many demands on our time and attention. However, self-care is essential for our well-being and can actually make us better caregivers in the long run. In this post, we'll discuss why self-care is important and provide some tips for making it a priority.

Research has shown that self-care can have a range of benefits for mothers. For example, a study by Ledermann and colleagues (2018) found that mothers who practiced self-care had lower levels of stress and depression, and higher levels of life satisfaction. Another study by Madsen and colleagues (2016) found that self-care was positively associated with maternal self-efficacy, or the belief in one's ability to be an effective parent.

In addition to these psychological benefits, self-care can also benefit our physical health. For example, getting enough sleep, eating a healthy diet, and exercising regularly can all help us maintain good health and prevent chronic diseases.

Tips for Prioritizing Self-Care

Making self-care a priority as a mom can be challenging, but it's important to remember that taking care of ourselves is not selfish - it's necessary. Here are some tips for prioritizing self-care:

  • Schedule it in: Just like you would schedule in a doctor's appointment or a playdate, schedule in time for self-care. Whether it's a yoga class, a massage, or just some quiet time to read a book, make it a non-negotiable part of your schedule.
  • Get support: Don't be afraid to ask for help when you need it. Whether it's asking your partner to watch the kids for an hour or two, or hiring a babysitter so you can have a night out, getting support can make it easier to prioritize self-care.
  • Start small: You don't have to overhaul your entire routine to make self-care a priority. Start small by incorporating small acts of self-care into your day, such as taking a few deep breaths or going for a short walk.
  • Be kind to yourself: Remember that self-care is not about being perfect - it's about taking care of yourself in the best way you can. Don't beat yourself up if you miss a workout or indulge in a treat. Be kind to yourself and focus on the positive steps you are taking to prioritize self-care.
