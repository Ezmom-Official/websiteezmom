5 Reasons Why You Should Read To Your Kiddo!

Although there are plenty of children's books on the market, sometimes it can be easy to skip a bedtime story. After an evening of your kiddo trying to stretch their bedtime, it might come to your mind to put them to bed without reading to them. However, reading to your kids has a few important benefits that might make you think twice about reading to your kids the next time they go to bed

  • It helps your connection to your kid. Cuddling and showing affection to your little one is a great way to strengthen your bond. Providing story time before going to bed is a great way to have some quality time, which will ultimately improve your connection with their kid. Even before your kid is able to understand what you are saying, your voice will soothe them!
  • Another reason why you should read to your child is because it increases their vocabulary. By reading many stories about different topics and from different writers, you are able to expose your kiddo to a large range of words from an early age on. This can help with their literary skills and vocabulary in the long term, which makes this fun way to learn super important!
  • Reading increases emotional intelligence. By reading stories your kid can relate to, they can develop their sense of empathy. Stories are also a great way to provide good examples for your kid, and they can help with establishing values you want to expose them to.
  • Kids often seem like they are full of imagination and creativity. Reading can help with that! By listening to the words, the kids can visualize the characters and setting, which helps them with their imagination! This activity also helps with brain development, which is always a nice added bonus!
  • After a long and busy day, you might be longing for a few moments of peace, but there are chances your kiddo doesn’t agree to that. Stalling their bedtime or staying awake for a long time can fill your evening up, but thankfully reading also helps with that! Reading helps to calm your kid down and to make them more relaxed. Because of this it is easier for them to wind them and fall asleep!

The list above is really only the tip of the iceberg when it comes to the benefits of reading, but hopefully they are enough to inspire you to snuggle up to your young one and enjoy some time together getting lost in an imaginary world!
